7. KEY ARCHITECTURAL DECISIONS
   - Reading scripts are useful for CHUNKING/ROUTING (which pages to send for which fields) but NOT for helping the LLM understand ambiguous structure
   - Vision-based extraction solves the real problem: PDFs are visual documents, text extraction destroys layout meaning
   - Tiered extraction approach: text heuristics for easy fields, vision for hard fields — the ground truth comparison determines which tier each field belongs to
   - The ground truth files are the bottleneck for everything else
