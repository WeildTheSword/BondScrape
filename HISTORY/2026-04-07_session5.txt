ARCHITECTURE NOTES
------------------

  click sounds on DOMContentLoaded via event delegation

  — no external audio files

- Pipeline data fetched from nos_test_set/ (ground truth JSON + extracted text)
- Firms section uses IIFE to avoid polluting global scope, loads firm JSON
  files and localStorage custom firms independently from sidebar firm selector
- Sequential scraper pipeline uses Promise-wrapped SSE EventSource listeners
